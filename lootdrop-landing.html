FAQ

How is this different from a normal event check-in tool?

LootDrop converts attendance into immediate on-chain rewards, not off-chain points trapped in one app.

Is LootDrop only for gaming use cases?

No. It is designed for IRL community growth, partner activation, conference quests, and verifiable attendance rewards.

Where does trust come from?

Claim rules live on-chain. Supabase is used for metadata and geospatial indexing, while chain state remains authoritative.
